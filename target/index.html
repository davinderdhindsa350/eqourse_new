About Us

Experience the world of learning, differently with eQOURSE.

eQOURSE is a downright rational, factual, and a comprehensive edtech platform, that presents a vision for learning and education, powered by technology.

Our strong educational content provides personalise learning to address every individual’s needs and interests as well as provide access to learning opportunities around the world. eQOURSE provides an extraordinary learning experience, with services such as Curriculum Planning, Study Material Development, Academic Digital Solutions, Teachers Training for K12, Entrance Examinations, and Preparation Material for IIT JEE (Main and Advanced), NEET, AIIMS. With our techniques, we aim to provide a world class learning experience with:

  • A scientifically designed curriculum.
  • A Guided learning pedagogy.
  • A One- Stop solution for all academic necessities.

Services

Our services are designed around increasing academic proficiency amongst students and help increase opportunities to practice skills in an authentic environment.

Curriculum Planning

Our planning and analyses are based to focus on learning outcomes, assessments, content and pedagogic requirement of a student’s overall curriculum success. Under this, we provide Teacher Training and management, Lecture notes and solutions and the entire academic planning mapped according to the holiday calendar.

Study Material Development

Our expertise lies in developing K12 and Entrance test preparation content. The customization varies from Board to Scholarship programs, Target exams to Coaching institutes and local needs to government scholarship exams. Our Study Material constituents of Chapter Modules, Daily Practice Problem (DPP’s) and Practice Test papers.

Academic Digital Solutions

Our HD quality video content provides students with a ‘Real Time’ classroom feel. The expert faculties make the videos engaging and enriching at the same time. We provide these lectures throughout the course as Pre-recorded videos and Live as well. All videos include Doubt sessions and Test Paper Discussion support.
